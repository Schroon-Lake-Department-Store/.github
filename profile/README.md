# Schroon Lake Department Store

**Your Adirondack General Store Since 1880s**

Welcome to the official GitHub organization for [Schroon Lake Department Store](https://schroonlakedepartmentstore.com) - a historic retail establishment located in the heart of the Adirondacks, serving locals and visitors for over 140 years.

## About Us

Schroon Lake Department Store is more than just a store - it's a piece of Adirondack history. Our building has served the community for over 140 years, and we're committed to preserving that legacy while embracing modern retail solutions.

### What We Offer

- Outdoor gear and equipment
- Clothing and apparel (men's, women's, kids')
- Footwear and accessories
- Local and regional Adirondack gifts
- Home decor and art
- Balsam and maple products
- Premium brands including Patagonia, Snow Peak, and Goodr
- And more!
